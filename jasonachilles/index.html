About

Jason Achilles is a multi-instrumentalist, producer, and analog artist known for transforming raw sound into immersive sci-fi soundscapes. Known for helping capture the first sounds on Mars with NASA’s Perseverance rover, Jason blends mad scientist tech obsession with post-progressive instrumental rock alongside drummer Forrest Mitchell. The duo creates music equal parts cinematic, experiential, and otherworldly, performed entirely live, no backing tracks, no pretense.
